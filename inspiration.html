. Inspiration

Hello, my name is Emily Zhao

These are the artists that inspire me

Michelle Phan

She is a makeup guru, artist, and entrepreneur on YouTube. She inspires me because we are very similar in that we both love makeup, art, and fashion. One key thing about her is that she doesn't see makeup as a way to hide under. On the contrary, she sees it as a way to express herself. Both of us love makeup because to us, it's like drawing, except it's on our faces.


Stan Lee

He was the creator of Marvel Comics and created so many superheroes, from the Fantastic Four, to the Avengers, to Spider-Man. He inspires me to chase my dreams of making my own superhero comics and making my superheroes as unique as I can. His own unique hero was Spider-Man, who was unlike any superhero at that time.


MyFroggyStuff

The channel is run by Latoya Moore-Broyles and her daughter Bella, often referred to as "Froggy, and Little Froggy" on the channel. I find inspiration from the channel because I love arts and crafts and often make similar things to the channel during my free time. I use MyFroggyStuff as a way to improve my crafts and as a challenge to try to make things from everyday objects, such as bottle caps or cardboard boxes.
